Our Services

Empowering education through AI literacy and consulting

AI Literacy Workshops & Consulting

We provide AI literacy workshops and consulting for educators, parents, and students. We help schools, families, and community groups understand how to effectively use AI in daily work and learning.

Professional Development

Workshops for Teachers

Comprehensive AI literacy training designed specifically for educators. We demonstrate practical applications of AI tools like Microsoft Copilot in classroom settings, helping teachers integrate AI effectively into their daily workflows and lesson planning.

  • Hands-on training with Microsoft Copilot and other AI tools
  • Curriculum integration strategies
  • Classroom management with AI assistance
  • Personalized implementation plans
Family Education

Seminars for Parents

Educational seminars that help parents understand AI technology and its role in their children's education. We provide guidance on supporting AI-enhanced learning at home and addressing common concerns about AI in education.

  • Understanding AI in education landscape
  • Supporting children's AI literacy development
  • Digital citizenship and AI ethics
  • Home learning enhancement strategies
Student Development

AI Literacy Sessions for Students

Interactive workshops designed to teach students responsible AI usage, critical thinking about AI-generated content, and practical skills for leveraging AI tools in their academic work and future careers.

  • Responsible AI usage and ethics
  • Critical evaluation of AI-generated content
  • Academic integrity with AI tools
  • Future-ready digital skills
Institutional Support

School & District Consulting

Comprehensive consulting services for educational institutions looking to develop AI policies, implement AI tools district-wide, and create sustainable AI literacy programs that serve their entire community.

  • AI policy development and implementation
  • District-wide training program design
  • Technology integration planning
  • Ongoing support and evaluation
Community Impact

Community Group Training

Tailored AI literacy programs for community organizations, libraries, and non-profits. We help bridge the digital divide by making AI education accessible to diverse community groups and underserved populations.

  • Accessible AI education for all backgrounds
  • Community-specific use case development
  • Digital equity and inclusion focus
  • Sustainable community partnerships
Custom Solutions

Specialized AI Implementation

Custom AI solutions for unique educational challenges. Whether you need help with specific AI tools, custom training materials, or specialized implementation strategies, we work with you to create tailored solutions.

  • Custom AI tool selection and setup
  • Specialized training material development
  • Unique use case implementation
  • Ongoing technical support
