What We Do

We are a group of Georgia Tech students who recognized the growing need for educators to understand and effectively utilize AI tools in their teaching practice. Our mission began with the simple idea that teachers shouldn't be left behind in the digital revolution, but rather empowered to harness these powerful technologies to enhance their educational impact. Through hands-on workshops and personalized training, we help educators integrate AI tools seamlessly into their daily workflow, making teaching more efficient and effective.
